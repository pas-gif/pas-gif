BMI Categories

BMI Range Category Description
Under 18.5 Underweight Below the normal weight range. May indicate malnutrition or other health issues.
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight Within the ideal weight range for most adults. Indicates a healthy balance between height and weight.
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight Above the normal weight range. May increase the risk of developing health problems such as diabetes and cardiovascular diseases.
30.0 and above Obesity Significantly above the normal weight range. Poses higher risk for various health issues, including heart disease, diabetes, and hypertension.
